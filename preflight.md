# Preflight Checklist

---

## Driver

### Pre-Move Start
- Slide FOD
- Check tires and pressures
- Check Oil Levels (Trans & Engine)
- Check Check Steering Levels
- Check washer Fuild
- Engine started and idling (2 min)
- Gauges checked and stable
- Fuel level confirmed
- Parking brake engaged until ready to move
- **Engine warm-up (2 minutes):**
  - Oil pressure normal
  - Idle smooth
  - No warning lights

### Driving Setup
- Mirrors adjusted (accounting for motorcycle carrier)
- Cameras working (rear + side)
- GPS/route set before departure
- Lights and signals tested
- Seat and steering position set
- Comms setup and paired (5 primary 7 alt) 

---

## Outside 

### Break Camp
- Shore power unplugged and stored
- Water hose disconnected and stored
- Sewer hose disconnected, rinsed, and stored
- Propane turned off at tank
- propane turned of for outdoor kicthen

### Exterior Setup
- Compartments locked w tabs and key
- Starlink put away.
- Steps retracted
- Awning secured
- Slide-outs fully in
- Leveling jacks raised w/ blocks removed
- Hitch fully inserted and pinned (locking pin preferred)
- Carrier stable and level
- Front wheel in chock
- Fork Saver installed 
- 4 straps tight (2 front / 2 rear)
- Suspension slightly compressed
- No strap slack, twist, or fray
- ramp installed 
- bike installed and secured

### Roll off
- Remove levelling blocks from tires
- Verify Clearances
- Dump
- Scout Departure
- RV departure notification to scout



---

## Inside

- Pets secured (crate/harness)
- Cabinets and drawers latched
    - Front Bed
    - Over Couch
    - over Sink
    - Under Sink
    - Refrigerator secured
    - Bedroom
    - Bathroom
    - Over dinette
    - Over Entrance
- Bed fully retracted
- Loose items stowed
- Windows and vents closed
- Appliances and fans off
- Snacks, drinks, and essentials ready for travel

---

## Scout Car / Lead Vehicle (Second Car)

### Driver Setup
- Fuel level sufficient for lead travel
- GPS synced with RV route
- Comms check and paired
- Communication method established (5 primary 7 alt)
- snacks
- water
- cameras clear

### Car external
- Roof Rack Secure
- Thule Secured & locked
- Oils Checked
- Fuilds Checked
