# RV Arrival Checklist

## Arrival
- Water fillup
- Wifi / Cell quick Check for work
- Scout does Bathroom and Neighbor checks

## OUTSIDE

### LEVELING & SETUP
- Set wheel blocks as needed
- set leveling blocks as needed
- Level RV front-to-back and side-to-side

### Exterior Safety Check
- Look for obstacles (posts, trees, uneven ground)
- Confirm no damage from travel
- Check tires for obvious issues
- Quick visual check of straps and doors
- Hitch still fully seated and locked
- Carrier level and stable
- No leaks or dragging parts

### PARKING
- Set parking brake
- Enable AGS
- Enable Antena for tv
- Transmission in park
- Extend Slide Slide

### POWER & CONNECTIONS
- Connect shore power surge protector only
- disable breaker and connect fully
- Verify power indicator is active and connect shore power
- Check for leaks at connection

### INTERNET CONNECTIVITY CHECK
- Connect GLi to Data source
- Verify signal strength in RV
- Test internet access on phone or device and establish VPN

### INTERIOR SETUP
- Unlock cabinets and drawers
- Re-open refrigerator
- Turn on water pump if needed
- Turn on appliances as required
- Open vents/windows if desired
